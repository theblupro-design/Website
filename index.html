Meet Our Founder

With over 30 years of experience in aquaculture, farming, and marine life studies, the founder of THE BLU PRO has dedicated his career to understanding the science behind nutritious and protein-rich seafood. His extensive expertise in seafood cultivation and quality management inspired a vision to bring truly farm-fresh seafood to families in metropolitan cities.

Driven by a passion for healthy living and a deep understanding of marine ecosystems, he explored advanced cleaning and packaging techniques that preserve the natural freshness, flavour, and nutritional value of seafood. His dream was simple — to deliver premium, protein-packed seafood from the coast directly to customers' doorsteps without compromising on quality.

Today, that vision has become a reality through THE BLU PRO, where freshness, hygiene, and trust come together to provide families with a superior seafood experience.
